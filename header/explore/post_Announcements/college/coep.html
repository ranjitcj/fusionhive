COEP

The College of Engineering Pune (COEP) is one of the oldest engineering colleges in India, established in 1854. Located in Pune, Maharashtra, COEP is renowned for its high academic standards, research excellence, and vibrant campus life. The institute offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and technology. COEP is known for its strong industry connections, state-of-the-art facilities, and emphasis on holistic development through various clubs, events, and technical festivals.
