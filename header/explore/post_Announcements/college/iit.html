Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur)

The Indian Institute of Technology Kanpur (IIT Kanpur) is one of India's premier engineering institutions located in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 1959, IIT Kanpur has been at the forefront of technological education and research in the country. The institute offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various engineering and science disciplines. With a strong emphasis on research and innovation, IIT Kanpur houses several state-of-the-art laboratories and research centers.
