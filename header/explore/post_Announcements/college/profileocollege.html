TSSM'S BSCOER

TSSM's Bhivarabai Sawant College of Engineering and Research (BSCOER) is located in Narhe, Pune. The college is committed to providing quality education in the field of engineering and technology. It offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines. The college has well-equipped laboratories, experienced faculty, and a focus on research and development.
